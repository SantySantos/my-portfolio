Self Reflection

Description

Play as someone going through their average day. begin the day in your bathroom getting ready and end the day at your desk at work. Yet you must be careful not to lose yourself, for if you go too long without seeing yourself, you might cease to exist.

Project Info

Role: Gameplay/UI Programmer
Team size: 7
Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Tools: Perforce, Blender, Maya
Duration: 48 hours
Date: April 2025
Awards: 3rd Place Winner BE Interactive Game Jam 2025
Theme: If You Can't See It, It Doesn't Exist

My Experience

This was my first in-person game jam I experienced. It was a great opportunity to work under a tight deadline and challenge my skills as an aspiring video game programmer.

Working in the same space as others felt inspirational and helped me realize how much communication and decision-making matter in an enviroment with such limited time. I had to adapt quickly and submit my tasks on time to assure proper gameplay for the player and a completed game, while undestading how neccesary it is to simplify ideas to fit the time limit.

I was responsible for the save system of our game, we wanted the theme to be fully integrated into the game in all ways possible, so what you already saw and can't see it anymore, shouldn't be there, even if the game is closed. This system helped me understand how different parts of a game connect together, how imporant is it to have an organized system, and how every mechanic in a game can affect the overall game experience.

This game jam gave me more confidence in my skills and made me realize how much can be achieved with the right mindset, how important communication, good planning and collaboration are and how they can lead to great results. Self Reflection was a great learning experience with a mind-blowing output.
