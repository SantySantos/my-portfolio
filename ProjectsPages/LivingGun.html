Living Gun

Description

Living Gun is a side-scrolling action game where you are a gun and must traverse and shoot your way across this war. Switch between four different types of guns to kill enemies, level up, gain stats, and defeat unique bosses.

The game features:

  • 4 different guns
  • 3 levels, each with a different environment
  • 4 unique bosses, each with their own unique attacks
  • Level system: Kill enemies to gain levels and automatically increase stats
