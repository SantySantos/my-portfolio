Killer Journalist

Description

Play as a raccoon journalist gathering evidence for the Raccoon Times. Uncover the secrets held within a crime scene, discovering what was left behind. Carefully frame your shots to capture the story YOU want exposed. There is more lurking behind this scene than meets the eye.
