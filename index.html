Scattered Time Puzzle

Gameplay Programmer and Level Designer

4th Semeter Final Project — 3rd Place Winner School Game Jam

Built in 1 month, Scattered Time was created in a team of only 3 members with the theme "Back to Square One" at Collège LaSalle in Montreal.

Scattered Time takes place in a maze where everything goes back in time every 60 seconds. The player needs to discover its secrets, collect items to unlock new areas, and find the 8 portal shards to fix time and scape the loop.
